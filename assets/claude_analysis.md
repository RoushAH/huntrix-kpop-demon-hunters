# Rumi Character Analysis (from Cover Photo)

Looking at the left character (Rumi) in the Cover Photo:

## Hairstyle
- **ONE thick purple braid** starting from a high ponytail position
- The braid is MASSIVE - nearly as thick as her arm
- Hangs down her back, extending to waist level
- Purple color (#9966ff range)
- Small turquoise/cyan bead accent at the braid end
- Gold hoop earrings

## Face & Expression
- Large expressive anime eyes (typical chibi style)
- Confident, friendly expression
- Small determined smile
- Bold defined eyebrows

## Outfit
- **Bright golden yellow cropped bomber jacket** - this is THE signature piece
  - Black trim/accents on collar and edges
  - Rolled or pushed-up sleeves showing forearms
  - Cropped to show midriff
- **White or very light purple crop top** underneath
- **Black high-waisted shorts** with prominent **gold belt buckle**
- **Black platform boots** with:
  - Yellow/gold laces
  - Gold accents/buckles
  - White folded-down socks visible at top

## Weapon
- **Large glowing purple energy sword**
- Blade length approximately equal to her torso height
- Bright purple/magenta glow effect
- Held casually in one hand, tip near ground

## Proportions (CRITICAL)
- **Chibi/super-deformed ratio: approximately 1:2.5 (head:body)**
- Head is LARGE relative to body (arcade beat-em-up style)
- Short legs, compact torso
- This is NOT tall anime proportions - it's CUTE and SQUAT
- Similar to: Streets of Rage, Final Fight, River City Girls character select screens

## Color Palette
- Purple: #9966ff (hair, sword glow)
- Yellow/Gold: #FFD700 range (jacket, boot accents, belt)
- Black: shorts, boots, jacket trim
- White/cream: crop top, sock tops
- Cyan accent: bead on braid

## Art Style Notes
- Bold black outlines (pixel art style)
- Vibrant, saturated colors
- Minimal but effective shading
- Cute K-pop idol aesthetic
- Retro arcade game character art feel
- High contrast, readable from distance

## KEY DIFFERENTIATORS
What makes this Rumi and not generic purple-haired girl:
1. ONE massive braid (not two, not ponytails)
2. Yellow jacket (NOT purple, NOT pink)
3. That specific purple sword glow
4. The chibi proportions (not tall fighter proportions)
5. Gold accents everywhere (earrings, belt, boots)
